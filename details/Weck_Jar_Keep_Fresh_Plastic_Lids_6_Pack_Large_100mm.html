Weck Jar Keep Fresh Plastic Lids

These 6-pack Weck jar lids are perfect for preserving freshness and extending the shelf life of your food. Made from durable, BPA-free plastic, they provide a safe and odor-resistant seal to keep your jars secure in the fridge or freezer.

The flexible design adapts to the contents inside, allowing for slight expansion during freezing and reducing the risk of glass breakage. Ideal for use with specific Weck jar models, they ensure a snug fit and efficient storage organization.

With a snap-on construction, these lids are easy to open and reseal, making them a practical addition to your kitchen. Plus, they are dishwasher-safe, so cleaning is a breeze and maintenance is effortless. These lids are specifically for refrigeration and freezing, not for canning, making them a reliable and versatile solution for everyday food storage.
