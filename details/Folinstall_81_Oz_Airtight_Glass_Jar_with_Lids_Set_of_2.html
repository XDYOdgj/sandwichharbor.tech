Folinstall 81 oz Airtight Glass Jars

A perfect blend of style and functionality, these glass jars are designed to keep your food fresh, secure, and organized. Whether you're storing dry ingredients or liquids, Folinstall offers a reliable, modern solution that fits seamlessly into your kitchen.

What Makes These Jars Special?

Equipped with an airtight design featuring a silicone gasket, these jars are built to prevent moisture and air from entering. This makes them ideal for keeping your spices, grains, and snacks in top condition for longer.

The wide mouth, measuring 4.3 inches, allows for easy filling, cleaning, and even the removal of items like sourdough starters or jars of pickles. The raised part on the lid also makes the jars easier to handle, especially when reaching into tight spaces.

With a durable, BPA-free plastic screw-on lid, these jars are not only sturdy but also safe for long-term use. They can be placed in the fridge, kitchen, bathroom, or laundry room with ease, and are dishwasher safe for extra convenience.

Why Choose Folinstall?

These mason jars with lids are a great addition for any home cook or food enthusiast. Their square shape is perfect for saving space in pantries, countertops, or storage cabinets, while the scale marks make measuring ingredients and tracking capacity a breeze.

Whether you're storing homemade cookies, pickles, or even kombucha, these versatile food storage containers offer style, functionality, and long-lasting performance. They are ideal for food storage and designed for kitchens that want to optimize every corner.

Key Features
  • Airtight design with silicone gasket for leakproof storage
  • Wide mouth 4.3\" opening for easy access and cleaning
  • Dishwasher safe plastic lid with sturdy screw-on mechanism
  • Scale marks for clear measurement and capacity tracking
